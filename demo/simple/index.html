Simple integration

This page uses one button inside <wc-speech>, wired to command="--playpause". There is no speech bar, voice picker, or speed control — defaults only.

Point target at the content to speak (#article). Keep that target separate from the speech tool when you can. The component skips its own subtree when collecting text.

Press 🗣️ Læs op to hear this article. Use the same button to pause, or press Escape to stop. Mark text in the article to open a Læs højt popover and hear only the selection.
